Di Cui(崔笛）

I am now an Assistant Professor at Xidian University. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Xi'an Jiaotong University, China, in 2019, under the supervision of Prof. Qinghua Zheng and Ting Liu. My official homepage: https://faculty.xidian.edu.cn/CUIDI

My research interests are but not limited to Software Refactoring, Software Architectural Analysis, and Big Data-driven Analysis.

Email：cuidi@xidian.edu.cn
